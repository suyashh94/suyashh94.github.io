Workflow‑Guided Exploration (ICLR 2018)

DOM‑aware RL on real websites · ← Back to VLA

Summary

WGE encodes high‑level task workflows that constrain exploration, improving sample‑efficiency on real, complex websites.

Key Ideas

  • Leverage structure (forms, buttons, navigation) as priors.
  • Constrain policy search with workflow graphs.
  • Demonstrates improvements over naive RL on real sites.
